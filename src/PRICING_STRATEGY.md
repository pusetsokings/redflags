## Option 2: Pay-What-You-Want (High Ethics, Lower Revenue)

### Model
- App is fully featured and free
- "Support Development" button in Settings
- Suggested amounts: $2.99, $4.99, $9.99, or custom
- One-time payment or recurring support

### Pros
- Maximum accessibility
- Builds goodwill and loyalty
- Appeals to users' values
- No features locked behind paywall

### Cons
- Unpredictable revenue
- Typically 5-10% conversion rate
- Harder to sustain long-term

---

## Option 3: One-Time Purchase ($9.99)

### Model
- Single purchase unlocks everything forever
- No subscriptions or recurring charges
- Optional "tip jar" for additional support

### Pros
- Users prefer one-time payments
- No ongoing billing concerns
- Simple and transparent

### Cons
- No recurring revenue for ongoing development
- May limit accessibility for those in financial distress
- Lower lifetime value per user

---

## Option 4: Freemium with Sliding Scale (Most Ethical)

### Free Version
- All core features (moment logging, basic red flags, emergency resources)
- Limited AI chat (5/day)

### Premium Tiers
- **Student/Financial Hardship**: $1.99/month (honor system)
- **Standard**: $4.99/month or $29.99/year
- **Supporter**: $9.99/month (helps fund free access for others)

### Why This Works
- **Financial inclusion**: Nobody priced out due to finances
- **Honor system**: Trust-based approach for vulnerable populations
- **Community support**: Those who can afford more help others
- **Ethical leadership**: Sets industry standard for safety apps

## Trial Period

### 14-Day Free Premium Trial
- Give all new users full premium access for 2 weeks
- Long enough to:
  - Log multiple moments (see patterns emerge)
  - Try advanced features
  - Use unlimited AI chat
  - Experience full value

- Standard 7 days may not be enough to log sufficient moments
- Relationship patterns take time to emerge
- Demonstrates confidence in product value
- Reduces friction for vulnerable users

### The "Hospital Test"
> If someone showed up at a hospital emergency room, would we charge them extra for this feature?

If the answer is yes, it's okay to be premium.
If the answer is no, it must be free.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why isn't FlagSense completely free?
FlagSense's core safety features are and always will be free. Premium features help fund ongoing development, support, and keep the free tier robust. We believe in sustainable, ethical monetization.

### What if I can't afford premium?
Select the Financial Hardship tier ($1.99/mo) or apply for a scholarship through our partner organizations. No questions asked, no verification required.

### Do emergency features cost money?
Never. Emergency hotlines, basic red flag detection, moment logging, and PIN lock are always free.

### Can I switch between monthly and annual?
Yes, at any time. Annual saves 50%.

### What's your refund policy?
14-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. We want you to feel confident in your purchase.
